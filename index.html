Shogo App

Bringing Art to People and People to Art.

Role: UX/UI designer. Timeline: 3 weeks

Current event management and social media apps fall short of giving users the proper tools to organize, book, and promote events at local and affordable venues.

Artists and performers need a way to organize popular and successful events in their area because renting space is expensive, self-promotion is daunting, and booking people is complicated. We believe creating a centralized platform for emerging artists to organize and promote events will achieve more engagement with local audiences and allow artistic communities to thrive.

We developed Shogo to give creatives and their fans a simple, centralized platform to produce their own events, book local spaces, and grow their live audience with real-time updates, automated promotion, simple scheduling, and easy payments.

User Research Findings

We started off our research with wanting to understand what’s important for amateur and semi-professional artists that are event organizers. We wanted to get insight on their most common pain points and strategies, so we focused on three main objectives:
